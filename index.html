About

I am a Research Fellow at the Wellcome Centre For Human Neuroimaging, University College London.

My research specialises in new methods in measuring brain activity using magnetoencephalography (MEG), a non invasive method of measuring biomagntic fields.

Publications

I have authored/co-authoured papers on multiple subjecs, from decomposing MEG data to reveal dynamic networks, Bayesian modelling of the hippocampus and even using weareable MEG to image the spinal cord. A full list of publications can be found on Google Scholar
